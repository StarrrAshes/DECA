Competition Prep

Preparing for DECA Events

Tips and strategies to help you perform your best at DECA competitions.

Tip

Know Your Event Format

Each DECA event has specific requirements — role-plays, prepared presentations, written plans, or online simulations. Read the DECA Guide thoroughly to understand the format, scoring rubrics, and time limits for your chosen event.

Tip

Practice Role-Plays

For role-play events, practice thinking on your feet. You'll typically get 10 minutes of preparation time before a 10-minute presentation with a judge. Work with classmates to simulate the pressure of timed presentations.

Tip

Study the Career Cluster Exams

Many events include a career cluster exam worth up to 50% of your score. Use DECA's practice exams and study the knowledge areas for your cluster — marketing, finance, hospitality, or business management.
